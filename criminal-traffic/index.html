Common criminal and traffic cases

These situations often involve deadlines, court dates, and paperwork that varies by state. The sections below provide a quick overview and link to dedicated pages (coming soon).

Criminal Defense

Criminal defense covers a wide range of charges, from misdemeanors to serious felonies. These cases can involve arrest procedures, bail, court appearances, and evidence review. Early legal guidance can help clarify options, obligations, and what happens next.

DUI / DWI / Drug Charges

DUI, DWI, and drug-related charges can affect driving privileges, employment, and criminal records. These cases often involve traffic stops, field tests, breath or blood testing, and strict response timelines. Missing a deadline can increase penalties or limit available options.

Learn more →

Traffic Tickets

Traffic violations can include speeding, reckless driving, license issues, or failure to appear. Even routine tickets may lead to fines, points, higher insurance costs, or license consequences. Some citations may be contestable depending on the facts and local rules.

Domestic Violence / Assault

Domestic violence and assault allegations often involve protective orders, court restrictions, and criminal exposure. These situations can be urgent and may affect housing, contact with family, and future court outcomes. Understanding immediate obligations and the process early can reduce uncertainty.

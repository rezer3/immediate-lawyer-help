Wrongful Termination

Wrongful termination claims may involve discrimination, retaliation, or firing that violates a contract or public policy. These cases often depend on documentation such as emails, performance reviews, and written policies. Timelines can be short, especially when agency filings are required.

Employment

Employment law can include wage and hour disputes, harassment, workplace accommodations, non-compete issues, and severance review. The right approach often depends on the type of claim, the employer’s policies, and what records exist.
